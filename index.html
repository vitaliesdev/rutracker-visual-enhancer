Browser extension

RuTracker Visual Enhancer

Posters, fast filters, configurable columns, infinite scrolling, and polished Light/Dark themes for supported tracker result pages.

Visual results

Posters, summaries, availability indicators, author cards, and clearer dates make long result lists easier to scan.

Fast navigation

Filter loaded rows instantly, keep only seeded items, collapse advanced search, and optionally load the next page automatically.

Your layout

Choose visible columns, three poster sizes, Light or Dark themes, and one of five interface languages.

Local by design

No analytics, advertising, account, external API, or developer-operated data collection. Preferences and cache stay in the browser.

Independent and unofficial
